# Notices

Agent Governance Suite (AGS) 2.0 Public Edition is a source-available
engineering-governance framework for multi-agent development workflows.

AGS is free to download, inspect, modify, and use for personal, research,
educational, and internal engineering work. Commercial use is allowed for your
own internal development operations, but AGS may not be repackaged, rebranded,
resold, hosted, or offered as a paid wrapper product without prior written
permission.

AGS includes original Rust governance tooling, task-card protocols, execution
policy logic, release-boundary checks, memory-capsule templates, and public
skill-governance workflows.

Some development-workflow ideas and optional skill recommendations are inspired
by, adapted from, or interoperable with the Superpowers agentic skills
framework. Superpowers is separate third-party work by Jesse Vincent and Prime
Radiant, licensed under MIT. See THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md for attribution.
